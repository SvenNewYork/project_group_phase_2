We're not just ANY cake shop run by hipsters out of Brooklyn. We sell cookies too. And pies.
So why not pop in and try a treat? Or two?
(Seriously, please come. Our rent is crazy expensive.)

Way, way back...

(at least five years)

...a bunch of entrepreneurial kids banded together
to score the biggest-ever stash of candy.
But after hours of neighbourhood door-knocking and polite smiles,
the kids pooled their earnings...
to find nothing but bucketfuls of Tootsie Pops and stale candy corn.
WTF, right?

Bonding like crystallized caramel over this holiday betrayal,
these kids made a pact:
that one day, children would no longer wait a whole year
for a pile of "ya basic!" candy.
One day, there would be a place for every sugary taste,
with a confectionary for every craving,
all under one candy-coated roof.
(Spolier: those kids are us. Holla!)

Fast-forward to today:
We're older (totally 18, trust us), smarterer, and ready to conquer the world
with business skills learnt from YouTube and massive loans from our parents.

We had a dream, and we built it.
'Cause WGAF about cavities or diabetes?
Life is for #living!

Namaste,

Dagger, Persephone, and Braille.
